About

My name is Lucas Rock, I am a member of Project Team 27 and my project is an informational page about the rock band the Red Hot Chili Peppers. I chose to do my project on the Red Hot Chili Peppers because I have been a fan of their music since I was little because my parents were always playing their songs quite frequently in the car and around the house, so I thought, "why not pay homage to one of my favorite bands." In my attempt to pay homage to such an iconic band I chose to highlight what I thought was some of the important stuff, so I decided to do their discography, awards, and their upcoming tour dates, I would have done more but as a solo project that would be too much to handle. Some lessons I learned from this project are that making a website is not as easy as I thought it would be, and that time managment is important. Time managment meaning working on the content first before going overboard with additional functions that might end up causing more harm than good.
